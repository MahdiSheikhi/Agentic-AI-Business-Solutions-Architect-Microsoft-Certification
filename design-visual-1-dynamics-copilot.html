Copilot in Dynamics 365 Sales

1 · Business terms — the configuration path

Direct exam bullet. Business terms are not configured in the Sales app — the path runs through Copilot Studio. Walk the pipeline left to right.

Where business terms liveCopilot Studio → environment → agent “Copilot in Dynamics 365 Sales” → Knowledge → SalesSpecificQnA — never inside the Sales app settings.
Glossary= term + description. Teach Copilot org language: “overdue task” = open task with due date < today.
Synonyms= alternate column names, so “deal size” can resolve to your custom revenue column.
When to design business termsacronyms · custom fields · custom ownership models · complex business rules that Copilot keeps misreading.
Propagation & reachtakes ~15 minutes after Publish; the same glossary serves Copilot in D365 Sales and the Sales agent in M365 Copilot.
Salesforce-connected variantglossary rows go in the Dataverse CopilotGlossary table; synonyms are unsupported with Salesforce CRM.
💡 Hook: “Copilot misunderstands our org's terminology in Sales” → glossary + synonyms in Copilot Studio (SalesSpecificQnA). Any answer configuring terms inside the Sales Hub is a distractor.

3 · Connectors for Copilot in D365 Sales — two doors for external data

Direct exam bullet. Both doors bring external data into seller experiences — know which door fits which scenario.

Door 1 — Custom connector + connector action

  • Built in Copilot Studio, enabled by a Sales admin
  • Ingestion takes up to 7 days
  • For your own external systems' data in Copilot chat

Door 2 — Partner-application insights

  • Via Power Platform connectors
  • Partner records/activities surface in context — e.g. DocuSign agreements beside the opportunity
  • For ISV/partner apps sellers already use
Custom connector + connector actionthe build-it-yourself door: Copilot Studio connector action, Sales-admin enablement, up to 7-day ingestion before data appears.
Partner-application insightsthe ready-made door: partner Power Platform connectors surface external records (DocuSign et al.) inside seller context.
Design cueboth doors are for external data; Dataverse data needs no connector — it's already the grounding source.

Admin home = Copilot Service admin center. The environment prerequisite comes first; then each feature has its own numbers.

Prerequisite switchPPAC “Enable AI-powered Copilot features” per environment, then configure under Productivity in the Copilot Service admin center.

Form-summary boundarycase-form summaries are D365 Customer Service-only; in Contact Center—embedded (non-Microsoft CRM) a case-summary card appears in the Ask a question pane instead.

6 · Channels & the unified-routing pipeline

Every conversation enters on a channel, flows through two routing stages, and lands on a queue.

📞 Voice — PSTN via Azure Communication Services, or bring-your-own-carrier / direct routing 💬 Chat 📱 SMS / social ✉️ Email 👥 Teams 🔌 Custom via Direct Line ☎️ Third-party telephony via Channel Integration Framework 2.0
Channel inventoryvoice (ACS PSTN or BYO carrier/direct routing) · chat · SMS/social · email · Teams · custom via Direct Line · third-party telephony via CIF 2.0.
Unified routing = two stagesclassification (rules + ML: skills, urgency, intent) → assignment (priority + skills/capacity/availability).
Routing flavorspercent-based routing, overflow, preferred-rep, and intent-driven routing.
Intent-driven routingthe Customer Intent Agent mines intents into a library; intents map to queues — “route by what the customer wants.”

7 · Copilot Studio agents on Contact Center channels — the integration anatomy

Direct exam bullet. The agent sits in a workstream ahead of human queues; when it escalates, context must travel with the call.

Placement patterncreate in Copilot Service admin center or Copilot Studio → connect to the omnichannel instance → add to a workstream ahead of human queues.
Voice/IVR feature setDTMF + speech, silence detection, SSML, barge-in, external speech grammars, transfer to rep or external number, multilingual.
Voice hard numbers1-hour bot-call limit; context passes to external telephony via SIP UUI / SIP X-headers.
Handoff with contextescalation passes full history + context variables (case ID → routing rules, macros, record linking); rep availability APIs let the agent check queues before escalating.
Extensions & caveatsinstall the D365 Copilot Studio extension (+ telephony extension); voice handoff needs both environments in supported geos, and auth variables don't pass on voice handoff.

Designing the three agent types

8 · Prompt-and-response · task · autonomous — the design ladder

The exam names exactly these three types. Each step up adds capability and governance load.

Prompt-and-response agentanswers by retrieving and synthesizing knowledge. Design concerns: grounding quality, permissions, citation behavior.
Task agenttakes defined actions in a conversation (knowledge + action tools). Design concerns: tool descriptions, authentication model, error/fallback paths.
Autonomous agentruns multi-step processes on event triggers. Design concerns: triggers, guardrails, human-in-the-loop approvals, audit, cost.
Before any agentfixed rules-based process → Power Automate / code; static Q&A over fixed documents → classic RAG. Agents earn their cost only on ambiguous, multi-step work.

9 · Autonomous agent design rules — the guardrail checklist

Straight from Microsoft guidance. Scenario questions test whether you apply all of these, not just some.

Narrow scopeexplicit authority boundaries — the agent does one process, not “whatever helps.”
Quality in, quality outhigh-quality instructions and data before autonomy; sandbox testing and gradual rollout.
Human approval for critical actionshuman-in-the-loop gates on anything costly or irreversible.
Least privilegethe agent's identity gets only the access the process needs.
Validate trigger authenticitye.g. sender checks on email triggers — untrusted triggers are an injection door.
Guardrails, fail-safes, auditlogging and monitoring on by design, with a stop path.
The three pitfallstriggers missing context (record ID, identity) · over-firing triggers burning Copilot Credits · ignoring platform quotas.
